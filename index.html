What is real here

Runs, balls, dot balls, dismissals, innings phases, bowlers, opponents, countries, cities, venues, and match dates come from Cricsheet's men's international JSON archives.

True line and length is not present in Cricsheet scorecards, so the app does not guess it. Bowler-type conclusions use a transparent style map, with unknown styles left visible in the data. CWC 2027 squads are not official yet.

Pressure score combines real dot-ball rate, strike-rate restriction, and outs, adjusted for sample size. Player pools are ODI projections; weakness conclusions use Tests, ODIs, and T20Is. CWC 2027 squads are not final.
